You no longer have to look “silly” when cycling around the city.

Hovding is the new bicycle helmet that you can wear around your neck like a scarf. It may seem just like a clothing apparel, but it has an airbag integrated ready to protect you when necessary.

It contains sensors and accelerometers that constantly monitor the movements of the cyclist and as soon as they detect a change in the speed and angle, a movement which patterns match the profile of a crash, it deploys the airbag and protects his/her head from all angles.

It also has a big on/off button at the front that lets you deactivate it and wear it away from your bike without the risk of deploying the airbag while you’re walking.

It’s that simple. When riding a bike, protect your self with style.

Don’t worry about your safety, just wear Hovding and let it worry for you
